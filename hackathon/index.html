Shift Hackathon

Shift Hackathon is a code sprint during which teams of up to five programmers have 24 hours straight to build an awesome new app. After they have successfully survived the crazy caffeine-fueled race to build their app, they are given a couple of hours to rest and then they have have additional 3 minutes to pitch their project in front of the judges and mentors

Theme

The Shift Hackathon will be open themed, meaning you can build any app you like. Bare in mind though that there will be API partners that will be offering prizes of their own so be sure to integrate as much of them as possible!
